How it Works (Unit 1)

1. Initiation: You specify the recipient and amount.

2. Hashing: The transaction data is hashed (SHA-256).

3. Signing: Your Private Key is used to encrypt the hash, creating a Digital Signature.

4. Broadcasting: The transaction is sent to the network (Pending pool).

5. Validation: Miners use your Public Key to decrypt the signature and verify the hash matches the data.
