Blockchain Applications (DApps)

Real-world use cases demonstrating the power of decentralized immutable ledgers (Unit 2).

Trade Finance

Smart contracts hold funds in escrow until shipment is verified, reducing fraud.

Supply Chain

Track the provenance of goods. Data is immutable and timestamped.

Health Insurance

Patients cryptographically authorize hospitals to access medical records.

Risks & Limitations

Scalability

Public chains like Bitcoin process ~7 TPS. Visa processes 24,000 TPS. Every node must process every transaction.

Energy Consumption

Proof-of-Work (PoW) consensus requires massive computational power, leading to environmental concerns.

Latency

Block times (10 mins for BTC) mean transactions are not instantly final. Network congestion increases wait times.
