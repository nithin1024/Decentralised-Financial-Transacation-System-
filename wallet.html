Identity on Blockchain

Unlike traditional systems, blockchain does not use usernames or passwords. Instead, mathematical Key Pairs are used:

  • Public Key: Acts as your address. Others use it to send you funds.
  • Private Key: Used to create Digital Signatures. Proves you own the funds.
Security Note: If you lose your private key, your funds are permanently lost. If someone else gets it, they can spend your funds.
