Ecosystem Applications

Industry-specific modules utilizing the hybrid blockchain infrastructure.

📦 Global Supply Chain

Architecture: Uses Layer 3 Solidity Contract (`supplychain.sol`).
Mechanism: Products are minted as unique identifiers. Logistics providers call `updateStatus()` executing state changes via Web3.

> AddProduct("Pharmaceuticals_Batch_1")
> UpdateStatus(1, "In Transit", 0xCarrier)

🏦 Decentralized Banking

Architecture: Hybrid model.
Mechanism: Traditional ledgers are replaced by the Layer 1 custom chain. Cross-border settlements execute without intermediary clearinghouses, settling instantly upon PoW consensus.

🏥 Health Insurance Data

Architecture: Layer 1 Custom Chain (Zero-value transactions).
Mechanism: Patient records are hashed (SHA-256). The hash is broadcasted to the chain. Providers query the chain to verify data integrity without exposing raw PII (Privacy by design).

⚠️ Network Risk Assessment

  • Scalability Limits: L1 simulated chain processes sequentially. High volume leads to mempool congestion.
  • Energy Profile: Layer 1 PoW consensus requires extensive CPU cycles, simulating high real-world energy burn. Layer 3 (Ethereum PoS) mitigates this.
  • Data Availability: Storing large files directly on-chain is prohibitive. HDFE enforces IPFS hashing conventions for payload referencing.
